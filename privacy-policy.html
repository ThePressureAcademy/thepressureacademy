Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

The Pressure Academy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website thepressureacademy.com and The Pressure Planner mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, belt rank, training goals
  • Training Data: Sessions logged, techniques practiced, sparring notes, journal entries
  • Lifestyle Data: Sleep hours, stress levels, nutrition, hydration, family time
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Feature usage, session duration, app interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Calculate your daily Pressure Score
  • Provide personalized training recommendations
  • Generate insights about your progress
  • Improve the AI Coach responses
  • Send relevant updates and notifications (with your consent)

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use:

  • HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
  • Encrypted databases for stored information
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Data Aggregation & AI Training

Important: We may aggregate anonymized training patterns across all users to improve the AI Coach. This means:

  • Your individual journal entries are NEVER shared
  • We analyze patterns like "triangle success rate from guard" across all users
  • This makes the AI smarter for everyone without compromising privacy
  • You can opt out of data aggregation in Settings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of data aggregation
  • Update or correct your information

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: For email signup forms (email only)
  • Google Play/App Store: For app distribution
  • Vercel: For website hosting

8. Children's Privacy

The Pressure Planner is not intended for users under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.

Address: Queensland, Australia

10. Australian Privacy Principles

We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). As an Australian business, we are committed to handling your personal information in accordance with these principles. You may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe your privacy has been breached.
