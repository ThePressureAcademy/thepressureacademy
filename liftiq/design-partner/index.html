Who this is for

Serious operators with real allocation complexity.

  • Commercial Pilot candidates willing to review fit, access, support scope, and dispatcher control before broad feature claims.
What a design partner gets

Structured working sessions, not vague interest.

A bounded briefing, walkthrough, pilot suitability review, and clear current-state boundaries.

What Pressure Systems gets

Real workflow truth from operators who actually run the pressure.

What this is not

Not a procurement-ready rollout.

No production-readiness, autonomous dispatch, permit decision, lift engineering, live integration, or measured ROI claim.

Proof sequence: public pages establish the category and boundaries. The fictional sample preview is shared only after serious fit exists.
First engagement shape

A credible first engagement has clear boundaries.

DispatchTalon should enter through a real operational problem, a private briefing, and a pilot suitability conversation.

1

Executive briefing

Pressure Systems covers current product truth, fit logic, and the decision-support boundary.

2

Pilot suitability review

The operator and Pressure Systems test whether data, workflow pain, and sponsorship justify a controlled pilot.

3

Phase-1 operational focus

Labour-only and plant + labour workflows, worker import, job brief import, scheduling, SmartRank, audit, and asset/crane review flags.

4

Phase-2 pathway only if justified

Later discovery may cover integrations, routes, permits, documents, inboxes, calendars, or finance questions. Those remain planned work only.

Credible to say now

  • DispatchTalon is a founding-partner pilot with a browser-based dispatcher workflow.
  • DispatchTalon supports labour-only and plant + labour modes, company setup, worker import, job brief import, scheduling, SmartRank, audit, asset context, and tenant-isolated pilot access.
  • DispatchTalon is positioned for controlled testing, founding-partner, and commercial pilot conversations.
  • Founding-partner pilot pricing is discussed after fit check and workflow review.

Do not claim

  • That DispatchTalon is production-ready or enterprise-ready.
  • That DispatchTalon is live-integrated with dispatch software, Xero, MYOB, external calendars, GPS / telematics, email inboxes, OCR, or PDF parsing.
  • That DispatchTalon approves permits, engineers lifts, guarantees compliance, or functions as a regulatory reporting platform.
  • That DispatchTalon has proven ROI or live customer outcomes.
