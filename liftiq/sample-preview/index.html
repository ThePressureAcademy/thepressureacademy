Watch list

Pressure signals the allocator should not need to hold in memory.

  • 3 operators above the 44-hour weekly watch threshold.
  • 2 candidates blocked on site-specific access or induction gaps.
  • 1 support truck already committed to a later metro corridor job.
  • 1 crane class conflict if the port lift overruns beyond 08:15.

Why it matters

The value is not a pretty score. It is visible decision support.

  • Ranking is explainable against fit, fatigue, credentials, and timing.
  • Hard blocks surface missing requirements without pretending they are optional.
  • The reasoning stays visible enough for a dispatcher, ops lead, or branch leader to challenge it.
