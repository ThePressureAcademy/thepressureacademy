Current pilot-backed modules

Current capability stays separate from future ideas.

DispatchTalon is pilot-stage software. These modules are backed by the current workflow; deferred ERP, finance, route, permit, OCR, inbox, calendar, and telematics ideas stay out of the headline.

Module group Status Current truth Boundary
Operating modes Pilot-backed Companies can run labour-only or plant + labour workflows. Not generic HR software or a full fleet platform.
Our Business setup Pilot-backed Company-specific requirement catalogues filter credentials, VOCs, civil, rail, energy, equipment, and transport options. One-off requirements stay job-scoped unless promoted later.
Asset Register Pilot-backed Plant + labour companies can track multiple assets under the same equipment class using asset or plant numbers. Not maintenance, telematics, GPS, depreciation, or asset compliance management.
Worker and job intake Pilot-backed Worker import, safe archive, job brief import, scheduled jobs, and timezone-aware windows are live in pilot scope. Not payroll, OCR/PDF parsing, inbox ingestion, or autonomous job creation.
SmartRank + gates Pilot-backed Explainable SmartRank uses CredentialGate, FatigueGuard, availability, schedule, and preference signals before allocation. Decision support only. The dispatcher remains the decision-maker.
Crane, counterweight, transport Pilot-backed Plant + labour mode supports crane model, travel state, counterweight gap, support transport, and road-access review flags. Not permit approval, route optimisation, road legality, or lift engineering.
Audit and metrics Pilot-backed Requirement selection, asset selection, SmartRank, allocations, overrides, worker archive, and counterweight review can write audit events. Not external regulatory reporting or advanced analytics.
Multi-company pilot access Pilot-backed Tenant-isolated pilot workspaces support company login, data separation, access status, and first-login rotation. No public self-serve signup or company switcher claim.
Future / not-yet modules Separated ERP replacement, payroll, invoicing, quote builder, permit automation, GPS, telematics, OCR, inbox ingestion, and external calendar sync are outside current scope. Do not promote until real product surface exists.

Structured review before dispatch

DispatchTalon shows checks and review prompts. It does not approve permits, engineer lifts, or guarantee outcomes.
