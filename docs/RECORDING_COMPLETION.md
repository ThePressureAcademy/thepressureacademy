### Recording Tips for Efficiency
- Batch recordings by topic to reduce context-switching
- Aim for "good enough to ship" quality — perfection delays launch
- If any content section is not essential for v1, defer it to a post-launch update
- Record in a quiet, consistent environment for audio quality
- Keep recordings under a consistent time length for packaging
