Pricing

Clear support, clear pricing

Mastery Method is designed to be transparent. You'll always know what you're paying for, what's included, and what each step is designed to do. No hidden fees, no lock-in contracts, no pressure to add more than your family needs.

How pricing works

You're paying for a system, not for hours

Traditional tutoring charges by the hour. The more your child needs, the more you pay — with no end point in sight. Mastery Method is structured differently.

The Clarity Assessment is a one-time investment that produces your family's first Teaching Blueprint. The Clarity Pathway is a monthly subscription that keeps that blueprint current, gives you guided support at home, and includes regular check-ins with Kirsty. Targeted 1:1 Sessions are only added when specific gaps need direct teacher-led work.

The model is designed so that your family becomes more capable over time — not more dependent on paying for more sessions.

No lock-in contracts. The Clarity Pathway is month-to-month. Stay as long as it's useful, leave when your family is ready.
No hidden costs. Every tier is clearly defined. You'll never be surprised by what's included or what's extra.
No pressure to upgrade. Targeted 1:1 is recommended only when it will make a meaningful difference — not as a default upsell.
