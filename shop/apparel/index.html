The work behind the work

Restraint is the design.

The locked mark

One locked Pressure Tested mark and wordmark carry the whole drop. No auxiliary marks, no sub-brands, no team badges. If it is not the mark, it is not on the garment.

The colour system

Four earth tones govern Drop 01: Clay Brown, Faded Olive, Sand, and Washed Stone. Core garments each own one tone. Women's colour assignments publish with the campaign board.

The silhouette set

Ten silhouettes, no filler: a structured hoodie, compression rashguard, grappling shorts, and boxy tee in the core, plus six women-specific cuts spanning two hoodies, a longline rashguard, a high-waist short, and two tees.

Print restraint

Print zones and patch placement are locked on the technical board and publish with sampling. The default is tonal and quiet. No billboard energy.

Fabric and construction

Fabric weights and construction specs publish with sealed samples. Nothing gets claimed before it is measured on an approved garment.

Sampling status

Drop 01 is at technical-specification stage. The next gate is sealed samples, then field testing, then final measurements and pricing. Each gate publishes here when it clears.

Sizing

Four families, measured properly.

Hoodies, rashguards, shorts, and tees size differently, and the women's cuts size differently again. Each family gets its own measurement table instead of one generic chart.
