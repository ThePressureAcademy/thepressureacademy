The work behind the work

Restraint is the design.

The locked mark

One locked Pressure Tested mark and wordmark carry the whole drop. No auxiliary marks, no sub-brands, no team badges. If it is not the locked identity, it is not on the garment.

The colour system

Four earth tones govern Drop 01. Clay Brown carries the core hoodie and two women's pieces. Faded Olive carries both rashguards. Sand carries both shorts. Washed Stone carries the core tee and two women's pieces.

The silhouette set

Ten silhouettes, no filler: a structured hoodie, compression rashguard, grappling shorts, and boxy tee in the core, plus six women-specific cuts spanning two hoodies, a longline rashguard, a high-waist short, and two tees.

Print restraint

The technical boards define restrained front, back, patch, hem, and neck zones. The removed auxiliary identity is excluded. Cultural artwork, names, and meanings remain gated pending provenance review.

Fabric and construction

The supplied technical packs now govern the development specifications: heavyweight cotton fleece, poly-elastane compression fabric, 4-way stretch woven shorts, and heavyweight combed cotton tees. Product cards show the intended fabric and construction. Final claims still wait for approved sealed samples.

Sampling status

Drop 01 is at technical-specification stage. Development size boards are available below. The next gate is sealed samples, then field testing, final measurements, pricing, and release approval.

Sizing

Four families, measured properly.

Hoodies, rashguards, shorts, and tees size differently, and the women's cuts size differently again. The supplied development tables are published as accessible HTML, with missing grading stated plainly.
