Clarity Call Free

For families who want to understand if this is right for their child.
A free, no-obligation conversation with Kirsty. She'll listen to what's happening for your child, ask about what you've noticed, and help you understand whether Mastery Method is the right fit — and what the best next step would be. No hard sell. Just an honest conversation.

What's included

  • 15-20 minute conversation with Kirsty
  • Understanding of your child's current situation
  • Clear recommendation on next steps
  • No obligation, no pressure
What happens next: If Mastery Method is a good fit, Kirsty will recommend a Clarity Assessment as the next step.

Clarity Assessment One-time

For families ready to understand exactly where their child is and where support should focus.
A structured, teacher-led assessment across the four clarity pillars: Thinking, Communication, Learning, and Action. Kirsty identifies exactly where your child gets stuck, why, and what kind of support will make the most difference. This assessment produces your family's first Teaching Blueprint.

What's included

  • Structured assessment across all four clarity pillars
  • Detailed parent report with findings and recommendations
  • Your family's first personalised Teaching Blueprint
  • Clear next-step guidance from Kirsty
What happens next: Your family can continue with the Clarity Pathway for ongoing guided support, or use the blueprint independently.
Request a Clarity Assessment

Clarity Pathway Monthly

For families who want ongoing guided support, visible progress, and practical tools for home.
The Clarity Pathway is the core of Mastery Method. It keeps your family's Teaching Blueprint current, gives you structured home activities each week, and includes a monthly check-in call with Kirsty so support stays targeted and relevant. This is a support system — not just more sessions.

Included every month

  • Updated Teaching Blueprint — personalised, current, actionable
  • Monthly parent check-in call with Kirsty (15-20 min)
  • Weekly home activity guidance — short, practical, pillar-aligned
  • Access to the parent resource library
Important: The Clarity Pathway does not include 1:1 sessions with your child. Targeted 1:1 Sessions are available as a separate add-on when needed.
Start with a Clarity Call

Targeted 1:1 Sessions Add-on

For children who need focused, expert-led support on specific clarity gaps.
Sometimes a child needs direct teacher-led work on a specific barrier — task comprehension, written expression, or self-checking habits. Targeted 1:1 Sessions are booked when that precision is needed. They are strategic and purposeful, not an ongoing commitment.

How it works

  • Live, teacher-led sessions with your child
  • Focused on specific clarity pillar gaps
  • Booked per session or in small bundles
  • Recommended by Kirsty based on your child's blueprint
Important: Targeted 1:1 is not included in the Clarity Pathway. It is a separate, optional layer of support — used only when direct expert input will make the biggest difference.
Discuss with Kirsty
What most families do

The typical pathway

Most families start with a free Clarity Call, move into a Clarity Assessment to get their first Teaching Blueprint, and then continue with the Clarity Pathway for ongoing guided support. Targeted 1:1 Sessions are added only if and when specific gaps need focused work.

There's no pressure to commit to everything at once. Each step leads naturally to the next.

Why this isn't tutoring

The Clarity Pathway is a system, not a session schedule

Traditional tutoring sells time. The Clarity Pathway sells clarity, visibility, and guided progress — with support that continues between live touchpoints.

Weekly tutoring model

You pay for hours

  • Progress depends on showing up each week
  • Parent receives little or no visibility
  • No structured home support between sessions
  • When you stop, progress stops
  • More sessions = more cost, same model
Clarity Pathway

You gain a system

  • Progress is tracked across clarity pillars monthly
  • Parent receives a Teaching Blueprint and check-in call
  • Weekly activities keep support active between sessions
  • Family builds capacity that continues independently
  • Targeted 1:1 only when precision support is needed
